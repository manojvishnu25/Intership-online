🛡️ Password Security Best Practices
  • Use Unique Passwords: Never reuse passwords across different websites
  • Length Matters: 12+ characters are significantly harder to crack
  • Complexity: Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
  • Avoid Common Words: Dictionary words are vulnerable to attacks
  • Use a Password Manager: Store passwords securely instead of in your head
  • Enable 2FA: Add two-factor authentication for extra security
  • No Personal Info: Avoid birthdays, names, or phone numbers
  • Regular Updates: Change passwords after security breaches
